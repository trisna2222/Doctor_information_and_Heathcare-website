Dr. Nafrin Amin Trisna is a cardiologist with over 19 years of experience working in reputed medical facilities, including Apollo Hospital Dhaka, Medvarsity, Hyderabad, India, and Sajida Hospital Dhaka. she completed her MBBS from Rajshahi Medical College (RMC), Diploma in Cardiology from Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University Hospital (BSMMU), and Diploma in Emergency Medicine from Medvarsity, India, and Royal Liverpool Academy, UK. she has been an instructor of Basic Life Support and Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support at the American Heart Association, and an instructor of Basic Emergency Cure at WHO in India. Over the course of his career, She has participated in various local and international training and workshops, attaining crucial knowledge of his line of work.
